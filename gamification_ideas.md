# Gamification Ideas For ASHA Workers

Possible mechanics:

1. XP Points
   - Household visit = +10 XP
   - Immunization update = +20 XP

2. Daily Streak
   - Consecutive active days

3. Badges
   - 10 Visits Badge
   - 100 Families Registered Badge

4. Progress Bar
   - Shows monthly completion

5. Village Ranking
   - Friendly comparison

6. Challenges
   - Weekly mission targets

Important:

Mechanics should work offline.
